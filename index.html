About Me

I'm Devanand Ravindran

Hi, I'm Devanand! I'm a software engineer who gets genuinely excited about writing code that makes people's lives easier.

I love building backend systems with Java and Spring Boot, there's something deeply satisfying about crafting APIs that just work seamlessly. My experience spans from helping digitize Kerala's Legislative Assembly (pretty cool, right?) to creating systems that sift through thousands of resumes in seconds.

What really drives me is that moment when I see my code actually helping people. Whether it's a government official who can now process documents faster or an HR team that finds the perfect candidate in half the time, that's when I know I'm doing something worthwhile.

When I'm not buried in Spring Boot configurations, you'll find me learning something new, sharing knowledge with fellow developers, or probably debugging something that "should definitely work" but doesn't.

I believe the best software isn't just technically sound, it's built with real people in mind.
